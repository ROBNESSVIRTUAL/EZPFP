Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒

The short version: EZ PFP runs entirely in your browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never seen by anyone but you. We have no database of user photos.

Your Images

When you load a photo into EZ PFP, it is processed entirely on your device using your browser's built-in technology. No image data is ever transmitted to any server. When you close or refresh the page, all image data is gone. We cannot see your photos, access them, or store them in any way — technically or otherwise.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how people use EZ PFP. This collects anonymous data such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • General location (country / city — not your exact address)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you found the site (e.g. Google search, direct link)

This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve the product. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

EZ PFP itself sets no cookies. Google Analytics may set cookies to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. These are standard, anonymous analytics cookies.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration or login — ever
  • No email addresses or personal information
  • No photos or image data of any kind
  • No payment information
  • No data sold to third parties

Third-Party Services

EZ PFP loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log the request to serve the font files. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

EZ PFP does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The tool is a simple image cropper and collects no personal data from any user, regardless of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection practices that compromise your privacy without clear notice.
